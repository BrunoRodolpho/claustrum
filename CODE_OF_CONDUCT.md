# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), version 2.1.

In short:

- Treat everyone with respect
- Be kind, patient, and constructive in discussions
- No harassment, discrimination, or hostility
- Disagree on technical merits, not personalities
- Focus on what's best for the project and its users

Violations may be reported privately to the maintainers (see [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) for the contact channel). Reports are taken seriously and handled confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, commits, or contributions that violate this code, and to ban contributors temporarily or permanently for repeated or severe violations.
